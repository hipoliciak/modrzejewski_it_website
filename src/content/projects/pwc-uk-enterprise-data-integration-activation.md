## Overview

Led the integration workstream for a complex enterprise transformation, aligning upstream data engineering with downstream marketing and CRM activation.

## Key Scope

- Connected Databricks, SAP, and Salesforce Data 360 in a governed enterprise architecture.
- Defined layered data products from atomic ingestion through aggregate curation to CRM-ready outputs.
- Established integration standards supporting secure and auditable activation paths.
- Enabled coordinated execution across Data 360, Marketing Cloud, and Salesforce core platform.

## Results

- Improved data quality and trust through explicit layer ownership and governance controls.
- Accelerated activation readiness for campaign and CRM teams.
- Reduced cross-team handoff friction with clear integration contracts and shared operating model.
