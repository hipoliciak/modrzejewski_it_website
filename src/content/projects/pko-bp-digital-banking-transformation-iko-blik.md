## Overview

Contributed to a major digital banking modernization initiative focused on mobile payments and omnichannel customer experience.

## Key Scope

- Co-launched IKO and BLIK capabilities in collaboration with product, IT, and data stakeholders.
- Supported design and rollout of secure data exchange and integration patterns across banking systems.
- Ensured compliance-aware implementation through governance with risk and security teams.
- Helped align channel experiences and operational processes around reliable customer data flows.

## Results

- Enabled scalable, secure data flows supporting high-volume digital adoption.
- Strengthened cross-team execution between business, technology, and data functions.
- Improved readiness for future banking channel enhancements based on robust integration foundations.
