## Overview

This project involved a full Salesforce CPQ implementation for a manufacturing client with a complex product catalog of 50,000+ SKUs.

## Key Challenges

- Complex pricing rules with volume discounts and regional variations
- Multi-currency support across 12 countries
- Integration with SAP for real-time inventory availability
- Custom approval matrix based on discount thresholds

## Solution

Implemented Salesforce CPQ with custom Apex extensions for SAP integration, achieving a quote generation time of under 2 minutes versus the previous 2-day manual process.
