## Overview

This engagement established advanced CRM and marketing automation capabilities for Totalizator Sportowy based on the full suite of Salesforce products.

## Key Scope

- Designed the Salesforce platform architecture across CRM, Data 360, Marketing Cloud and Loyalty Cloud.
- Implemented zero-copy federation between Snowflake and Salesforce Data 360.
- Defined canonical data models and integration contracts for customer, interaction, and activation domains.
- Enabled hyper-personalized automated activation flows powered by a unified customer view.

## Outcomes

- Unified customer profiles across channels with governed synchronization patterns.
- Reduced data movement complexity with federation-first architecture decisions.
- Improved campaign relevance and operational alignment between digital and retail teams.
